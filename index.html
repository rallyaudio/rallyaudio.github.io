Product

Built for quick moves.

Pack-and-play profile

The full controller set folds into a single box, keeping all knobs, faders, and performance pads ready to deploy in under a minute.

Controller twins

Two mirrored decks pop up for cueing and looping. Stay symmetrical for muscle memory or split duties between two performers.

Rally chassis energy

Crisp planes, bold color cuts, and numbered deck zones give the hardware the same intensity as a service-park machine.

Partner-ready

Designed to be collaborative: brand-ready shells, shared performance profiles, and custom layouts for labels and events.

Design language

Ticket systems, rally cuts.

The form language borrows from takeout packaging, boarding pass typography, and Group B rally liveries. Surfaces stay minimal while labels, serial blocks, and hard geometry keep controls legible fast.

  • Compact footprint for carry-on travel.
  • Touch points grouped for rapid transitions.
  • High-contrast labeling for low-light stage runs.

A real prototype is underway. The site is here to signal the vision to collaborators and potential partners.

The plan is to ship with flexible MIDI mappings so it can be tuned for Rekordbox, Traktor, Ableton, or custom rigs.

We are collecting interest and refining the hardware. Partners who want early access can reach out now.
