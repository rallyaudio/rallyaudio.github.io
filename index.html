FAQ

Quick answers.

What will you be offering?

We will be providing both completed units and DIY kits for the hobby enthusiast. 3D print your own enclosure if you want!

Will it work with X software?

While the Takeout Box has been designed for direct compatibility with Djay, it can be configured as a MIDI device with any software that allows for customization.

How much will it be?

We are working hard to bring the cost down, stay tuned for updates. We want to make it as cheap as possible without cutting corners on functionality.

When will it launch?

We hope to have a viable kit available in May of 2026 (subject to change).
